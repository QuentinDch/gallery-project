    Our Vision

    Studio Spectral is an Edinburgh-based still life photography studio, known for its personal approach to objects, light, and material. Specializing in capturing the essence of textures, light, and form, Studio Spectral creates unique, timeless images that reveal the beauty in the simplest of objects. The studio also collaborates with brands and individuals on custom projects, offering a tailored creative experience.
